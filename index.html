Kare-Kare Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 kg oxtail
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup shrimp paste
  • Eggplant, string beans, banana blossom

Instructions

  1. Boil oxtail until tender.
  2. Add peanut butter and season with shrimp paste.
  3. Cook vegetables separately and add to the dish.

Halo-Halo Recipe

Ingredients

  • Shaved ice
  • Leche flan
  • Sweetened beans (red beans, garbanzo beans)
  • Fruit cocktail
  • Ube ice cream

Instructions

  1. In a tall glass, layer the shaved ice, leche flan, sweetened beans, and fruit cocktail.
  2. Top with ube ice cream and a drizzle of evaporated milk.
  3. Mix all ingredients together and enjoy!

Leche Flan Recipe

Ingredients

  • 10 egg yolks
  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 1 can condensed milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Caramelized sugar for the base

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix egg yolks, evaporated milk, condensed milk, and vanilla extract.
  2. Pour the mixture into a caramelized sugar-coated pan and steam for 45 minutes.
  3. Let it cool, refrigerate, and serve chilled.
